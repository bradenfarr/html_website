The Shawshank Redemption

This is a great feel good movie. Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins) is sentenced to two consecutive life terms in prison for the murders of his
wife and her lover and is sentenced to a tough prison. However, only Andy knows he didn't commit the crimes. While there, he forms a
friendship with Red (Morgan Freeman), experiences brutality of prison life, adapts, helps the warden, etc., all in 19 years..
