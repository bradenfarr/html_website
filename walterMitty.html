The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

This movie is a great adventure movie, Walter Mitty (Ben Stiller), an employee at Life magazine, spends day after monotonous day developing photos for the publication.
To escape the tedium, Walter inhabits a world of exciting daydreams in which he is the undeniable hero. Walter fancies a fellow employee named Cheryl (Kristen Wiig) and would
love to date her, but he feels unworthy. However, he gets a chance to have a real adventure when Life's new owners send him on a mission to obtain the perfect
photo for the final print issue.
